Hide | Show | Toggle



How does hide, show, and toggle work? While you can see the text now, but if you click the hide button the text will disappear. To have the text reappear just click on the show button. The toggle button will act like the hide or the show button.

Slide Up | Slide Down | Slide Toggle



How does slide up, slide down, and slide toggle work? If you were thinking that it should work like hide, show, and toggle. You are right. There is a difference in the way the content disappears and reappears.

Fade In | Fade Out | Fade Toggle



How does fade in, fade out, and fade toggle work? Basically the same as the slide animation. The only difference is that there is a fading animation instead of a sliding animation.
